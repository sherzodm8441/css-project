A computer is a device that allows you to do many stuff, ranging from helping you to process tons of data all the way to helping you to find those dank memes. But what allows a computer to be able to do many different complicated tasks? Scroll down to find out.

CPU

The most important component of a computer is the CPU (short for Central Processing Unit). The primary function of this chip is to convert data input into information output. Think of this chip as the brain of the computer. Without the CPU the computer won't run at all.

GPU

The second most important component of a computer is the GPU (short for Graphics Processing Unit). It has a structure that is very similar to that of the CPU's, but as the name implies this chip is designed to process and render images, animations and etc. and output that data to your display.

RAM

You also need RAM (short for Random Access Memory) for your computer to run. RAM is the chip that stores all the code that is currently being executed by your computer's CPU and GPU. Without the RAM nothing will be displayed on your screen. Also increasing the amount of RAM your computer has will significantly increase the performance of your machine.

HARD DRIVE

The hard drive is the data storage device of your computer. When you pirate all of those FUNNY *** MEMES, they are usually stored in your computer's hard drive. You increase the performance of your computer by replacing the traditional hard drive with a Solid State Drive

MOTHERBOARD

The motherboard is the circuit board of your your computer that allows all the other components of your computer to work together to deliver to you the best exprience possible. The type of motherboard you have can either increase or decrease the porformance of your PC, it's best to choose the right one.

POWER SUPPLY

This one is a no-brainer but you have to have a power supply in your computer, for obvious reasons. Also it is very important to choose the right type of power supply since not all of them are created equal. A power supply that delivers the right amount of juice to your system will prevent decrease in performance.

CASE

You need a sturdy, well ventilated case to assemble your computer in. You obviously need a case because where else would you keep your exposed system.

MONITOR

Whenever we think of a computer we usually think of the monitor first. That is why, for your average comsumer, a computer is not a computer without a good display. Pairing up a nice display with your computer (preferably an OLED display) can really enhance the experience
